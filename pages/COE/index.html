Centers of Excellence

Innovation-driven hubs integrating academics, industry, and advanced cybersecurity research to build next-generation experts

Objectives

  • The objective of the CENTER OF EXCELLENCE in cyber security and digital forensics is to bring new solutions to the market which aims at solving problem statements of common internet user, law enforcements agencies and corporates too. Solutions should strictly contribute towards decreasing the number of cyber crimes in nation by all means.
  • Joint Projects: Both, first party & second party will work together to identify projects of importance in the area of Cyber Security and Digital Forensics and internship and training program. The work plan can be decided by mutual consent of both the parties.
  • 1 or 2 days virtual and offline workshop on cyber security and digital forensics will be conducted by CSI.
  • Solutions or services developed under the CENTER OF EXCELLENCE be eligible for start up or can be published as open source which can be used by individuals, corporates and law enforcements agencies respectively, with the mutual consent of both the parties.
  • Team of experts and empanelled mentors of Cyber Secured India will be reviewing the concept/project/idea and then will onboard it under Center of Excellence. Along with, mentoring on LIVE projects related to cyber security and digital forensics.
  • Center of Excellence will approach academics, government and private entities for implementation of the solution/ service innovated by the participants of the COE.
  • Center of Excellence will be working in collaboration with government and private entities and also with academics for implementing the POC solutions or services developed by the participants.
  • Designing of the syllabus as per current and upcoming industry standards and application of the same throughout the duration, as decided, shall be initiated by CSI.

Benefits

  • COE provide practical experience in real-world scenarios like identifying and mitigating security threats.
  • Students develop technical skills in areas such as network security, cryptography, forensics, web application security and ethical hacking.
  • Labs simulate real-world cyber threats, preparing students to handle actual security incidents effectively.
  • Labs encourage collaborative projects, mirroring the teamwork required in the cybersecurity industry.
  • Practical lab experience enhances resumes, demonstrating student’s readiness to contribute to cybersecurity efforts immediately after graduation.
