About Cyber Secured India (CSI)

Cyber Secured India (CSI) is a flagship initiative of the Feathers CSI Foundation, registered as a non-profit company dedicated to building a safer digital world.

Since 2014, CSI has been at the forefront of training, internships, mentorship, and capacity-building across the cybersecurity ecosystem. Over the years, we have empowered 2,00,000+ individuals through specialized programs in cybersecurity, digital forensics, and cyber awareness.

Our learners include:

  • School & college students
  • Law Enforcement Agencies
  • Corporate professionals
  • Government bodies
  • Cybersecurity enthusiasts across the world

Today, CSI is supported by a global community of 38,000+ members across 130+ countries, united by a shared mission — Cyber Security for All.
